Block explorers

Below is a list of third-party block explorer services displaying statistics about Ethereum proof-of-stake.

beacon.etherscan.io#

beaconcha.in#

beaconcha.in is an Agora chain explorer maintained by Bitfly.

Mirrors and Forks#

This sections lists open source mirrors of forks of original, major block explorers. Redot maintains two open-source mirrors that are forks of Bitfly's original work beaconcha.in at

  • ethscan.org
  • eth2.redot.com

Their respective Github repositories are:

  • nobd/ethscan-org
  • eth2-redot-com
